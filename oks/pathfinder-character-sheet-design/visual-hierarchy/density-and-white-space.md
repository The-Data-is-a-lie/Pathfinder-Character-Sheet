# Density and White Space

Pathfinder sheets will never be sparse. The goal is **structured density**: high
information per screen without losing group boundaries.

## Guidance

- Prefer **spacing and alignment** over heavy grid lines that cage every value.
- Use cards/panels with consistent padding; dividers must remain visible in both
  light and dark themes (thin gray rules often disappear on near-black).
- Leave breathing room around **Tier 1** numbers; compress description dumps.
- On paper, landscape orientation can buy horizontal modules; on web, max-width
  ~1000–1200px with responsive grids prevents endless horizontal sprawl.
- Open space invites annotation (sketches, custom trackers); over-ruled note
  areas discourage it.

## Digital-specific

- Virtualized or paginated long lists beat infinite walls of feats.
- Sticky tab bars and sticky combat headers preserve orientation while scrolling
  dense tabs.
