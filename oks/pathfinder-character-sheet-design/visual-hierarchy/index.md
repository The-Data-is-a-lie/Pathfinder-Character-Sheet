# Visual Hierarchy

Hierarchy decides what the eye hits first. On a Pathfinder sheet, the wrong
hierarchy turns a play aid into a tax form. Amplify play-critical numbers,
quiet labels, and use space and accent color deliberately.

## Concepts

- [typography for stats](/oks/pathfinder-character-sheet-design/visual-hierarchy/typography-for-stats.md) — scale and weight for names, modifiers, and body rules text.
- [accent and theme identity](/oks/pathfinder-character-sheet-design/visual-hierarchy/accent-and-theme-identity.md) — brand/system flavor without harming readability.
- [density and white space](/oks/pathfinder-character-sheet-design/visual-hierarchy/density-and-white-space.md) — pack information without losing scan structure.
