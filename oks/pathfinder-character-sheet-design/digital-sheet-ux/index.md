# Digital Sheet UX

Digital sheets can calculate, remember toggles, and put actions next to data.
That power only helps when automation is trustworthy and interaction design
matches tabletop tasks (roll initiative, full attack, track HP, flip a condition).

## Concepts

- [automation and calculations](/oks/pathfinder-character-sheet-design/digital-sheet-ux/automation-and-calculations.md) — compute modifiers; never block play when math is wrong.
- [conditionals and toggles](/oks/pathfinder-character-sheet-design/digital-sheet-ux/conditionals-and-toggles.md) — situational bonuses as explicit, reversible UI state.
- [task-oriented flows](/oks/pathfinder-character-sheet-design/digital-sheet-ux/task-oriented-flows.md) — design for “make this roll,” not “fill this form.”
- [editable vs. derived](/oks/pathfinder-character-sheet-design/digital-sheet-ux/editable-vs-derived.md) — what players type vs. what the sheet owns.
