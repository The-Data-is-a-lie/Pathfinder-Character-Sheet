# Change Log — Pathfinder Character Sheet Design bundle

2026-07-10 — bundle created

Initial authoring of the `pathfinder-character-sheet-design` OKF bundle for this
repository, following conventions from
[okf-bundles](https://github.com/kwcantrell/okf-bundles):

- Added bundle `index.md` and this `log.md`.
- Authored **information-architecture** (frequency-based layout, grouping related
  stats, progressive disclosure, session vs. identity data).
- Authored **digital-sheet-ux** (automation/calculations, conditionals/toggles,
  task-oriented flows, editable vs. derived fields).
- Authored **visual-hierarchy** (typography for stats, accent identity, density
  and white space).
- Authored **color-theory** (color roles/tokens, WCAG contrast, saturation and
  harmony).
- Authored **themes** (light surfaces, dark surfaces, dual-theme strategy, system
  preference sync).
- Authored **accessibility** (focus/keyboard/print, color-not-only signal).

Research sources include Roll20’s PF2e sheet redesign write-up, RPG character-sheet
design guides, Nielsen Norman Group dark-mode research, Material/Atmos dark-theme
guidance, and WCAG 2.1 contrast criteria.
